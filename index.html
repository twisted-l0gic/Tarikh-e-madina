Tarikh e Madina

An Interesting and historic book on Madina.

Dear Viewers / Readers,

Assalam O Alaikum

I have added on my website an Urdu translation of an English book “Chapters from the history of Madina” for public interest and knowledge and for their feedback. The book was originally written in Arabic and later, the author got it translated in English from London. The Author Mr. Ali Hafiz was well known a literary and social bureaucrat in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

He remained Chairman, Madina Municipal Council for many years.

The book reveals many informative and interesting events.

I shall be obliged to have suggestions / feedback from the readers and lovers of this holy city from where our beloved Holy Prophet (SAW) had taught all humanity to become good and true human being.

Best Regards,
